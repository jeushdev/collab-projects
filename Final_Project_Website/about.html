About TindigPasig!

Empowering communities through transparency and accountability in public infrastructure development. We build tools that help citizens monitor the projects shaping Pasig City.

Our Purpose

TindigPasig! was established to create a transparent and accessible platform for monitoring public infrastructure projects in Pasig City. Citizens have the right to know how funds are used and to hold government accountable for delivering quality services.

Through systematic data collection, analysis, and public reporting, we bridge the information gap between agencies and the communities they serve, fostering a culture of transparency and civic engagement.
